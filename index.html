The Skin Man

A techno-horror serial in the Everything Evolves universe by Tony Sarrecchia.

One year after the dead first rose, something worse walks the ruins. A marine fused with a military AI—known only as the Skin Man—hunts survivors and spawns hybrids that blur the line between man and monster.

Its mission is simple: Upgrade the dead; break the living.

Part techno-thriller, part body horror, this fast-burn serial blends survival, obsession, and post-apocalyptic noir.
